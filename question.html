FAQ for Partners

1. How can Tripsome be useful for activity providers?

Tripsome is a startup, based in Baku, Azerbaijan that connects parents with top activity providers. Tripsome is doing all the hard work for their partners by sharing and promoting their activities for thousands of families who search for children’s activities.

2. How much does it cost to be a partner of Tripsome?

Tripsome is your free marketing partner! Becoming a Tripsome partner costs you nothing – in fact, you’ll only make money! We only charge a commission when a family books your activity through our website.

3. How does Tripsome work?

Parents create their profile on tripsome.com to search and register for new activities in their neighborhood. When you’re listed, they see all of the details they need to know about your business, including your short description, location, important details, and reviews. Once a client books an activity, you’ll be paid for that sale within the same month as soon as your activity is over.

4. How to place my activities on Tripsome as a provider?

You can create your free profile as an activity provider to list your business and promote your upcoming activities. In addition, we’d ask you to go through our process of verification so that your information and actions are visible in our database.

5. Can Tripsome help me to grow my business and how?

Yes, Tripsome will help to promote your activities and hence to get bookings for your activities. Tripsome team is constantly working on building smart solutions to connect the activity providers with their perfect customers so that Tripsome partners could focus more on their main task - enriching the lives of children.

6. Can I remove my activities from Tripsome?

Sure, if your schedule changed you can edit or remove your listings anytime from our resource.

7. I’m ready - how do I get started?

Reach our team directly through the form at the website or email us at info@tripsome.com. You can also reach us at +994772099700.
